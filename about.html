Essence

ShivKaliBodh is a spiritual system to awaken and activate the power of God within us. By following the ShivKaliBodh spiritual processes, the energies of our subtle body purify, enhance and come in balance. Thus, the Aura and Chakra healing happens spontaneously in ShivKaliBodh. When our Mother Kundalini pierces our Sahasrara chakra, our union is established with the all-pervading divine consciousness and we experience divine bliss and protection all the time.

Divine Love and Godly Virtues

The all-encompassing selfless divine love and innumerable godly virtues awaken with the blessings of Maa Kundalini. Their manifestation in our thoughts, behavior and actions happens spontaneously with our spiritual growth in SKB. These are discussed in detail under the headings ‘Divine Love and Godly Virtues’ in this section.

Uniqueness

In SKB, Almighty God is worshipped in form as well as in formless. The focus is on connecting mankind with the all-pervading divine consciousness so that everyone’s life transforms miraculously. Human worship in any way is discouraged. SKB is process-driven and spiritually empowers all those who practice it. It does not make the followers dependent upon anyone.

Mission

Our mission is to guide everyone to live happily forever, without any problems and diseases, without any discrimination, falsehood, dishonesty, greed or human worship.

Vision

Our vision is to bring a positive change in this world and establish peace. This is possible only when humans are able to connect with God. Once the divine energy awakens within all, mankind will transform and peace will establish in this world.

ShivKaliBodh Publications

This section is a listing of all the publications by ShivKaliBodh. Each one offers a synopsis to help readers understand their essence.

ShivKaliBodh Prakram - Part 1, 2, & 3

This series outlines the Shivashakti Daily Havan, which is essential for purification of energies in ShivKaliBodh. Procedures are provided in Hindi and English; mantras are in Sanskrit, Hindi, and English. Written by BhaiGuru Mahesh and edited by Nipun Chandershekhar.

The Union

This book explains the philosophy of ShivKaliBodh. It is written by BhaiGuru Mahesh.

Anubhuti

This book is a collection of spiritual lectures by BhaiGuru Mahesh.

The Seven Spiritual Processes (Anubhuti Padhatis)

This book introduces the 7 spiritual processes of ShivKaliBodh which guide spiritual growth:

  • 1. Basic Spiritual Process (Aadhara Padhati)
  • 2. Second Spiritual Process (Dutiye Padhati)
  • 3. Third Spiritual Process (Tritiye Padhati)
  • 4. Fourth Spiritual Process (Chaturtha Padhati)
  • 5. Fifth Spiritual Process (Panchama Padhati)
  • 6. Sixth Spiritual Process (Agrima Padhati)
  • 7. Ascent Spiritual Process (Utthana Padhati)

The Four Weekly Pujas

This book outlines the weekly pujas that are part of the spiritual processes:

  • 1. Mahadeva Shiva ji puja
  • 2. Maa Kali puja
  • 3. Shri Ganesha, Shri Kartikeya & Shri Hanuman ji puja
  • 4. Shri Radha-Krishna ji puja

All instructions are in Hindi and English with mantras in Sanskrit, Hindi and English.

The Two Monthly Mahapujans

Essential monthly pujas described in this book:

  • 1. Mahadeva Shiva Maa Kali Amavasya Mahapujan and Havan
  • 2. Mahadeva Trishakti Purnima Manan Mahapujan

Again, procedures and mantras are explained in three languages.

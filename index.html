Welcome to ShivKaliBodh

Kundalini Yoga is the most mysterious and empowering aspect of spirituality. Maa Parvati, in Her black form as Maa Kali, resides within us as Maa Kundalini. She is our primordial mother. She is the shakti. Once She awakens, She purifies and energizes our subtle body comprising of the seven chakras, three channels, and ocean of illusion (bhavasagar). She also enhances are aura and protects us completely. She awakens the all-encompassing divine love and godly attributes within us, which soon manifest in our thoughts, behavior and actions. When She pierces our Sahasrara Chakra, She liberates us by establishing our union with the all-pervading divine consciousness. This is our union with God, Enlightenment, Liberation and Salvation. From the day our Maa Kundalini awakens, our lives begin to transform.

In Shivkalibodh, the devotees are blessed with the awakening of their Kundalini Shakti by the grace of Almighty Aadishakti Maa Kali and Almighty Mahadeva Shiva. When we attain the most evolved state of human consciousness through our union with God, we achieve the state of doubtless blissful Samadhi State, which is the state of remaining in continuous connection with God.

Enlightenment

During our journey in SKB, innumerable godly attributes awaken within us spontaneously and we overcome our negative traits. Initially, the primary virtues of the five elements of nature awaken within us. These virtues are selfless giving akin to bhumi tattva (earth element), magnanimity akin to gagan tattva (sky element), speed to our good deeds akin to vayu tattva (wind element), Enlightenment akin to agni tattva (fire element), and adaptability akin to neer tattva (water element).
